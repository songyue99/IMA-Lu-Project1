2021: Three-child Policy

The policy, whereby allows a couple to have three children, came out early this year. Some people support it due to concerns over population ageing and slow economic growth. Other people mock it because of the high pressure of raising even one child in the current society. How does birth policies actually affect population after their release? Let's explore now!
